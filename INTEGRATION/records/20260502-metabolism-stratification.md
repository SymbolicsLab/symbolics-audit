## What was integrated

The third face of the unified dynamic — the **maintenance face** — was recognized as the vault's existing concept of **metabolism** (the fold-remainder-unfold-refold cycle). Not a new term: a recognition that what the vault has long named "metabolism" IS the maintenance face of distinction-making, alongside mattering (driving face) and distinction-making (productive face).

Metabolism is universal across L1–L5+ but **mode-shifts at L4**. Below L4, only updating-mode is available (extension-adjustment within fixed apparatus). From L4 onward, contesting/revising mode becomes available (apparatus-revision through the modal/variational capacity that L4 makes possible). This locates L4 as structurally distinctive — it is where apparatus-revision becomes possible at all.

The vault's pre-existing §Updating vs. Contesting (in 01_Ontology) is the load-bearing distinction for this stratification, and is now carrying substantially more work than its size in the vault suggested.

## How the work landed

The work proceeded as a thinking-partner conversation, with structural challenges from the user reshaping the proposal at three key moments:

1. **First proposal** — three-moment pattern (in-act / held / convergence) parallel to mattering, applied to metabolism at every node. User pushed back: held-metabolism doesn't cohere because metabolism is inherently active.

2. **Second proposal** — metabolism is L4+ only; below L4, distinctions persist by fit/constraint but don't metabolize. User extended this with the Hannover interventionism connection (rule-interventions are L4 operations) — this confirmed the L4-character of revision-capacity.

3. **Third proposal** — locked picture: metabolism is L4+, three faces don't stratify uniformly. User then proposed reframe: maybe metabolism IS universal at L1–L5+, but the *mode* of metabolism shifts. The toddler-tiger case is metabolic by updating, even pre-L4. The actual distinction is updating vs. contesting/revising. This reframe stuck.

The final structure recognizes:
- Metabolism = remainder-driven response cycle that keeps distinction alive under pressure
- Two modes: updating (pre-L4 capable; extension-adjustment) and contesting/revising (L4+; apparatus-revision)
- Mode-shift at L4 because L4 is where modal/variational capacity comes online
- L4 "Update policy" renamed to "Modal apparatus / Revision policy" to free "updating" as the pre-L4 technical term
