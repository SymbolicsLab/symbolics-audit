## What was integrated

The May 1 mattering stratification articulated mattering across the nine-slot architecture using a three-moment pattern (in-act / held / convergence). The May 1 framing treated all three moments as facets of mattering. The work in this session surfaced structural findings the May 1 framing had not yet articulated:

1. **The temporality of the mattering/meaning duality** — they activate together at the two-body threshold, but their temporalities differ. Mattering is now-firing; meaning is persistence (held + convergence).
2. **The active/captured cross-correspondence** — a duality-internal asymmetry that cuts across both faces of the apparatus (structural face: distinction/representation; lived face: mattering/meaning). Active sides are now-firing and don't thicken; captured sides persist and accumulate articulation.
3. **The three-moment pattern is the duality articulated structurally** — in-act = mattering-side; held + convergence = meaning-side. The May 1 articulations of "held mattering" and "convergence mattering" relocate as *meaning-at-each-level* rather than mattering.
4. **Persistence requires partiality** — the deepest move surfaced. Wholeness can be lived but cannot be carried; persistence requires partial-articulated-capture; remainder is what persistence costs.
5. **Each ascension is meaning-formation** — the moment a new representation-kind comes online; distinct from in-level meaning's content-thickening within an established layer.
6. **Pedagogy structurally constitutive sharpened** — pedagogy just is externally-provided persistence-machinery for an articulation-layer the learner doesn't yet have, because convergence is dyadic by nature.
7. **Stale necessity generalized to stale representation** — substrate-open, partiality-invariant; three substrates (medium-inscribed, sedimented-as-capacity, community-held recognitive practice) exhibit the same shape; per-level instantiation. Framework's representation is post-cognitivist by design.
8. **Stale ascension as a new pathology shape** — persistence-machinery for L_{n+1} acquired without engine firing across the threshold.
9. **Cross-tradition debates as level-confusions** — referentialism vs inferentialism = L2 vs L3; analytic vs hermeneutic = L2/L3 vs L5+. Strong dissolution candidate.
10. **Wittgenstein's private-language argument generalized** to all nine slots via the convergence-moment claim.
11. **Lineage** — closest articulations identified: Ricoeur (event vs meaning), Whitehead (concrescence vs objective immortality), Stoics-Frege (lekta vs prophorai), Levinas (Saying vs Said), Brandom (commitment vs entitlement).

12. **Constitutive vs operational refinement** (added same session, late). Per-slot tradition mappings refined under the **constitutive vs operational** distinction. *Constitutive ascension theorists* articulate the conditions for a new layer to come online; *operational in-level theorists* work within an established layer. Refinements:
    - A(L1→L2) constitutive: Kant (forms of intuition + schematism); Sellars (myth of Jones); Husserl (typification); Wittgenstein (rule-following); Cassirer.
    - A(L2→L3) constitutive: Aristotle, Frege, Kant transcendental deduction, Hegel logic.
    - A(L3→L4) constitutive: C.I. Lewis (strict implication); Carnap (introducing intension); Kant modal categories; Leibniz; Hegel actuality/possibility.
    - A(L4→L5+) constitutive: Hegel master-slave; Heidegger Dasein; Kierkegaard; Sartre.
    - L3 in-level (operational): Brandom, Sellars space-of-reasons, Dummett — corrected from earlier conflation with constitutive figures.
    - L4 in-level (operational): Kripke (modal-semantic), Lewis (David), Stalnaker, Chalmers — Carnap's introduction of intension is A(L3→L4) constitutive, not L4 operational.
    - L5+ in-level (operational): Ricoeur (narrative), Honneth, Brandom (recognition), MacIntyre — distinguished from constitutive theorists Hegel/Heidegger/Kierkegaard/Sartre.
    Most major figures span slots — Sellars, Hegel, Kant especially. Map specific moves to slots, not whole traditions.

13. **Two constitutive-level dissolution candidates surfaced**:
    - *Kant's transcendental schematism as a one-body articulation of A(L1→L2)* — the apparatus needs the two-body version. Genuine dissolution candidate for Kant scholarship.
    - *Hegel's master-slave as the convergence-moment articulating itself* — vindication-with-clarification rather than strict dissolution. Strong candidate for German-idealism venues.

14. **Structural observation about philosophy as a discipline**. Lower ascensions (A(L1→L2), A(L2→L3)) are developmentally near-universal — contestable only via specialized transcendental, genealogical, or foundational work. Higher ascensions (A(L3→L4), A(L4→L5+)) are culturally and personally contested at the level of philosophical practice itself; their in-level traditions are richer because the ascension is *visible from where philosophy operates*. Philosophy's native operating-region is L4–L5+; what philosophy looks like from below requires specialized tooling.

## How the work landed

This was a generative thinking session, not just connection-work. The conversation opened with a sketch (per-level meaning content + traditions + cross-tradition level-confusions) that the user broadly accepted but flagged a clarification need: disambiguate mattering from meaning. Working through the disambiguation surfaced the temporality observation as a side-note, which the user picked up as the deeper finding. The user's articulation — "the temporality aspect of meaning vs mattering" — reframed two of the three moments (held, convergence) as meaning-side and one (in-act) as mattering-side. From there, the rest cascaded:

- The thickening asymmetry got its structural reason (persistence is the kind of thing in which articulation can build up; now-firing isn't).
- The substitution of "representation" for "content" surfaced the active/captured cross-correspondence cutting across the structural-lived divide.
- The deepest move — persistence requires partiality — fell out of the active/captured framing: only captured sides need to be partial because only captured sides have to persist.
- The post-cognitivist challenge (medium vs non-medium examples) led to clarifying that the framework's "representation" was post-cognitivist by design (substrate-open, partiality-invariant) and to the substrate-open generalization of stale representation.

The user's pace through the session: enthusiastic, mind-blown at the temporality finding ("Woah... this is profound, worth thinking about thoroughly"). Took the work seriously enough to challenge the medium-bias in examples — leading to the post-cognitivist clarification.
