## One vault-worthy advance

**Independent interveneability as empirical test.** The vault mentioned the connection to Woodward in passing. The EJPS paper develops it into a full methodological claim: value-interventions (vary inputs, hold policy) vs. rule-interventions (hold inputs, vary policy) provide an empirical test for the reality of policy levels. The demarcation (causation = value-sensitivity under fixed policies; non-causal dependence = rule-sensitivity under fixed base descriptions) is clean and testable.
