## Structural decisions

### 1. Fiction is the make-believe sub-mode within the symbolic apparatus

The May 7 cluster's framing was: fiction as a third mode of index-persistence parallel to fit (endogenous) and constraint (exogenous). Today's correction: fit/constraint name the two **substrate-modes of persistence** — physical (fit, endogenous) and symbolic (constraint, exogenous). Fiction is not a third substrate; it sits within the symbolic as a structurally distinctive sub-mode where the index is **bracketed**: the index refers within a frame to the world the frame produces, not outward to the field.

This correction came from the user's observation that the fit/constraint distinction reduces to physical world vs. symbolic world: "Spencer-Brown's draw a distinction presupposes power... endogenous distinctions take shape through fit... exogenous distinctions are held by symbolic apparatus." The two-substrate reading was implicit in the vault already (§Three Levels in 01_Ontology; §Two Sources in 04_Ecology); the user's framing brought it forward. Fiction-as-sub-mode within the symbolic is the structurally clean placement.

### 2. Fiction's structural shape: L4-style world-construction released by L5+ bracketing-frame

The bracketing-mechanism: a meta-commitment "operate in bracketed mode for the following content," which suspends the apparatus's default field-tracking commitment. This is reflexive (content about how to take content), so it lives at L5+. Mutual bracketing across bodies (author and reader both treating content as make-believe) is recognitive convergence on operating-mode, carried across bodies by institutional apparatus (the "A Novel" label, the bookstore section, narrative cues, genre conventions) — compressed dyadic trace operating at the meta-level.

The within-frame construction is L4-style (varies configurations, sets parameters, specifies content) but released from the actuality-accessibility commitments that bare counterfactual reasoning preserves. This is why fiction can range from possible (realistic novel) to impossible (fantasy): the L5+ frame releases the accessibility-commitment that L4 modal reasoning carries.

Within the constructed world, L1–L3 operate normally — indexed cuts, recognizable forms, inferential consequences. **Fiction has a layered slot-structure**: L5+ frame + L4 construction + L1–L3 within-frame operations.

### 3. Bracketing is constructive, not subtractive

The fiction-frame does not merely block reference to the field; it *constructs* a substitute thing-to-refer-to. The xenomorph's index has scope, locus, resolution, sameness — parameters supplied by the frame's act of specifying the alien. The frame functions as a field-replacement: a self-enclosed structure that within-frame indexing can anchor against, as ordinary indexing anchors against the field or the apparatus.

This sharpens the Spencer-Brown critique: "draw a distinction" presupposes a field to draw on; **fiction is the structural case where the drawing produces its own field** through bracketed index. (Light note added to §Fiction; full reframe of 04_Ecology §The Spencer-Brown Critique held for later.)

**Bracketing is selective, not total.** The frame cannot construct an entire world from scratch — pure fiction would be unintelligible. Most fictional content is partially borrowed from fit/constraint (creatures, schools, motives, social roles), and only specific elements are frame-original. **Pure fiction is structurally near-impossible**: the frame constructs deltas on the symbolic world's existing content, not a whole replacement world.

### 4. Artifact-mediated content-bracketing as fiction's distinctive persistence-feature

Fiction's persistence-mechanism is distinctive: an **artifact** (book, film, recording, performance trace) is actualized in constraint that *holds the content as bracketed*. The book is a real artifact in symbolic apparatus; Hogwarts stays bracketed within it. The bracketing survives across time *because* a real artifact carries it. Without the artifact, the imagining is daydream — bracketing dissolves with the firing.

This is the user's correction of an earlier overstatement on my part. I had framed fiction as content that "never gets actualized," conflating "content stays bracketed" with "nothing got actualized." The user clarified: the book IS the actualization (in constraint, as artifact); what stays bracketed is the content the book holds.

### 5. Three-way classification by who brackets

- **Fiction proper**: mutual bracketing. Author signals make-believe; reader takes up the bracketing. Both know.
- **Failed fit-claim** (including most superseded science — phlogiston, geocentrism): no bracketing. Producer and receivers all treat content as field-tracking. Resilience reveals the content didn't track; the structural mode was fit-claim throughout.
- **Lie**: asymmetric bracketing. Producer brackets internally (knows the content isn't field-tracking); presents as unbracketed. Receiver takes the content as fit-claim.

The bracketing-signal is what does the structural work, not whether the content tracks the field. Sincere flat-earth belief is a low-resilience fit-claim, not fiction.

### 6. Disambiguation: fiction vs. adjacent moves

Each of these operations can be performed inside or outside fiction; what distinguishes fiction is the bracketing-frame wrapping the content.

- **Abstraction**: subtractive (thinning the index, preserving the referential link). Orthogonal to fiction; both axes cut differently.
- **Stipulation**: constraint-extension. Math stipulation unbracketed; fiction-internal stipulation bracketed by the surrounding frame.
- **Counterfactual / modal reasoning**: L4 variation with accessibility-preservation. Bare counterfactual unbracketed; alternate-history fiction wraps the counterfactual in a frame.

### 7. Imagination is the speculative-suspension mode of creative becoming

Active firing, agent-internal, using the L5+ bracketing-release that fiction uses. **Parallel to deliberation**: both are speculative-suspension modes, differing in accessibility-commitment (deliberation preserves accessibility; imagination releases it).

The two modes **braid** in real generative work. The architect imagines wild forms (accessibility-released) and deliberates among real choices (accessibility-preserved). Different cashout-paths weight the braiding differently:
- Fiction-cashout is imagination-heavy (content stays bracketed)
- Fit-cashout is deliberation-heavy (content has to become real)
- Constraint-cashout sits in between

Imagination is agent-internal: each agent fires their own speculative construction. When that construction is mutually-signaled across bodies and persists in an artifact, it becomes fiction. **Imagination and fiction share the same L5+ bracketing-mechanism but differ on the convergence-side** (within-agent firing vs. mutually-signaled persistence across bodies).

Imagination can attach to any direction-species: speculative-suspension on becoming-toward-live (planning, creative imagining, fantasy about the future); on becoming-toward-foreclosed (regret, what-if, mourning-imagining); on becoming-in-ascension (anticipatory imagining of the level being crossed into). The directional-neutrality is what makes imagination a mode rather than a species.

Imagination does not require cashout. **Daydream** is imagination-without-cashout — same active firing, same bracketing, no commitment to produce anything.

### 8. Creativity is the productive kind of becoming

**Working definition**: Creativity is becoming through imagination, with any actualization-cashout (however ephemeral) into fit, constraint, or fiction.

Creativity is one of the activity-types of becoming, alongside executive and deliberative. What makes it structurally distinctive is its passage through the L5+ bracketing-release that imagination supplies, which lets the cashout produce content that was not already accessibility-preserved (new in kind, not merely new in instance).

The cashout sits on a **persistence-gradient**: utterance / talk (low — sound waves, listener's brief memory) → sketch / draft (medium) → finished work (high — artifact in constraint) → publicly-signaled work (very high — artifact + mutual recognition). All count as creative becoming. The threshold isn't a persistent artifact; it's any actualization at all.

**Three landing-places with distinctive bracketing-fates**:
- **Fit-cashout**: the imagined content IS the actualized thing. Bracketing released.
- **Constraint-cashout**: the imagined content becomes operative apparatus. Bracketing released into operative commitment (L4-revisable).
- **Fiction-cashout**: an artifact is actualized in constraint that holds the imagined content as bracketed.

### 9. Kind and property duality

User's observation about Louise Bourgeois: creativity is **both at once** — a *kind* of becoming the agent inhabits (the integrated character of continuous becoming when running in creative mode for sustained duration) AND a *property* certain actualizations have (those passing through imagination + bringing new-in-the-world).

The duality maps onto the active/captured pattern: kind is the integrated character that persists (captured-row); property is what individual firings exhibit (active-row). Same phenomenon, two scales.

The kind is **productive**, not just descriptive. The captured substrate of a creative-kind becoming conditions what creative-property actualizations can fire. Bourgeois could make her work because her whole becoming was set up for it — decades of substrate built through prior actualizations. Someone with thinner creative substrate can have moments of property, but the cashouts will be shallower. The kind conditions what the property can be.

Useful spectrum without sharp lines: Bourgeois (kind continuous, most acts have property, substrate vast) → scientist with one breakthrough (kind intermittent) → talker who imagines and verbalizes (property in each utterance, kind not sustained).

### 10. Creativity emerges from continuous becoming, not as discrete arc-event

The "creative arc" — input commitments built through prior actualizations → speculative-suspension via imagination → output cashout — is an analytical slice of continuous becoming. It can be drawn at any scale: short (wake up, look at tiger book, draw tiger) or long (the agent's whole life). All slices are legitimate; the longer ones surface what shorter ones clip.

Structurally, **there is no "creative phase" the agent enters and leaves** — just a kind the continuous becoming sometimes takes. The commitments conditioning any creative actualization are built as the wake of all prior actualizations bearing on the work. No separate commitment-building dynamic is required; the existing wake mechanism does the work.

The question of why a particular creative act takes the shape it does ("why drawing?" "why a tiger?") bottoms out in the agent's whole prior becoming. The short arc obfuscates this depth; the long arc surfaces it.

This corrected an earlier (worksheet) framing of creativity as a bounded arc with start and end. User's observation: "It's what's in the vault about the structure: actualizations are in the present, and they leave a wake which builds commitments. Can you have commitments toward something that was never lived in any way at all? I don't think so. The creative process is, I believe, a long process."

### 11. Activity-types of becoming as a new typology

The vault has direction-species (toward-live, toward-foreclosed, in-ascension) for becoming. Today's session added a cross-cutting typology by **activity-type** — the internal structure of how the gap is inhabited and cashed out:

- **Executive**: no speculative-suspension; commitment → actualization directly
- **Deliberative**: speculative-suspension with accessibility-preservation; commitment → deliberation → decision → actualization
- **Creative**: speculative-suspension with accessibility-release; commitment → imagination → actualization

These cross-cut direction-species. A single becoming has both a direction-species (where the gap points) and an activity-type (how it's inhabited and cashed). Creative-toward-foreclosed (Bourgeois processing her mother's death); contemplative-in-ascension (transformative meditation); attentive-toward-live (anticipatory listening) are real combinations.

Other candidate activity-types flagged: **contemplative** (sustained dwelling without external cashout), **practice / training** (capacity-building through repetition), **attentive** (oriented to letting external content shape commitments), **processing / integrative** (working through experience). Full mapping pending — see new §1 entry in pending-integrations.

### 12. Creativity is distinct from ascension / plastic act

The agent's L1–L5+ stack can stay stable while engaging in creative work; co-occurrence is possible but not constitutive. User explicitly distinguished these phenomena.

### 13. "Confined-to-imagination" instead of "failed creativity"

User's correction: failure-talk carries an evaluative load that doesn't sit at the structural level. The descriptive framing is "confined-to-imagination" — names what mode the becoming stayed in without implying it should have been something else.
