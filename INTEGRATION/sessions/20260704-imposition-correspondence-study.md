**The ratified point — the gate-diagnostic.** *Imposition = captured-drive that **bypasses, overrides, or has
captured** the agent's **owned** (reflexively-endorsable, de-naturalizable) mattering-gate.* Nourishment goes
*through* the gate (the museum moves you to paint because painting already matters to you); imposition goes
*around* it. **Gate-capture** (naturalization rewriting the gate so it assents) = the deepest form.

**Two framing corrections** (propagated to the `.pending-integrations.md` Imposition entry): **NOT
"foe-multiplied"** (friend/foe = an entangled valence, per-firing, not a partition of persons — the caregiver
imposes out of care); **NOT "mattering's negative pole"** (the *complementary*, valence-neutral second
drive-source — the flip is of *source*, not goodness). Imposition = the **second drive-source** (two-source
engine: mattering-pull + imposition-push); valence (gift/harm) is orthogonal; **constitutive before
pathological**.

**Mechanics.** captured→active *intensifies* the vault's existing `captured→conditions→active`
(conditioning→driving), NOT a grid reversal. Three driving-mechanisms: **depth** (`D`/sedimentation → habit),
**foreclosure** (filtering → ideology), **materialization** (multiplication→naturalization → the captured
trace becomes your field). Gain = captured-impetus *decoupled from the gate*; inverse to live mattering;
mattering-zero limit = **stale representation**. Two feltness channels: causal/material (violence — feelable
by anyone) vs symbolic/index (opacity-gated). Fourth cell = **self-imposition** (own groove vs endorsed gate;
single/multiplied sorts self- from social-imposition; internalization-via-depth bridges).
